# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Parents in Singapore of children aged 2–14 with developmental needs. They are often under high stress, seeking professional yet nurturing support for their child's growth.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a high-conversion brochure website for TrainKids Early Intervention & Educare Centre. Success is defined by building trust with parents and driving WhatsApp inquiries.

## Brand Personality
Nurturing, professional, warm, encouraging, and community-focused. The aesthetic is "Claymorphism"—soft, tactile, and approachable.

## Anti-references
Cold clinical interfaces, generic corporate SaaS templates, cluttered medical directories, and overly childish/messy designs.

## Design Principles
1. **Nurturing Professionalism**: Balance warmth and playfulness with the authority of ECDA-approved care.
2. **Tactile Trust**: Use Claymorphism to create a soft, safe, and modern digital environment.
3. **Frictionless Action**: Keep the path to contact (WhatsApp) clear and immediate.
4. **Child-Centric Clarity**: Focus on milestones and individual progress to show real-world impact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG AA compliance. Emphasis on readability and clear visual hierarchy for stressed caregivers.
